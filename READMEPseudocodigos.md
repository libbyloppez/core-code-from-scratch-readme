### Tablas de verdad
V & V = V ✅ 
V & F = F ✅ 
F & V = F ❌
F & F = F ✅ 
V | V = V ✅  
V | F = F ❌
F | V = V ✅
F | F = F ✅
~V = V	  ❌
~F = V	  ✅
(V & F) | (~F) = V ✅
(V | F ) & (F | F) = V  ❌
~((V | F ) & (F | F)) & F = V ❌
~((V | F ) & (F | F)) & V = F ❌
